# Localization Strategy

## Constraints

- German-first. German text is often 20-30% longer.
- 14+ languages implied. Layout must be resilient.

## Tiers

- Tier 1. German, English.
- Tier 2. Turkish, Polish, Romanian.
- Tier 3. Bulgarian, Croatian, Serbian, Hungarian, Czech, Slovak, Slovenian, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese.

## Rules

- German is the source language.
- Dates and times are locale-specific.
- Medical terminology must be professionally translated.
