# Product Vision and Positioning

## Use When

- You need the top-level story for decisions, scope, or critique.

## Vision

Trusted, accessible healthcare companion for dm customers. Single mobile experience for appointment booking, telemedicine, and prescription redemption.

## Positioning

- Not "another booking app". Full journey. Booking, consultation, prescription, delivery or local pickup.
- dm brand trust is the differentiator. Familiar retail brand extended into healthcare with credible partner integrations.

## Core Features (MVP intent)

- Appointment booking.
- Telemedicine consultations.
- E-prescription redemption. Online delivery or local pharmacy search.
