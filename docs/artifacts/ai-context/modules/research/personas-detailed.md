# Personas (Detailed)

## Sarah. Busy Working Mother (34)

- Motivations. Family efficiency, time saving.
- Pain. Waiting times, refill forgetfulness, app fragmentation.
- Needs. Refill reminders, quick telemedicine for minor issues, schedule health checks during shopping.
- Usage. 3-4x/week, iPhone, lunch breaks and evenings.

## Marc. Health-Conscious Professional (42)

- Motivations. Preventive care, data-driven, frictionless UX.
- Pain. Scheduling friction, waiting rooms, lack of quick advice.
- Needs. On-demand consults, preventive in-store checks, click and collect for OTC and supplements.
- Usage. 2-3x/week, iPhone plus Apple Watch, mornings and lunch.

## Helga. Senior With Chronic Needs (68)

- Motivations. Reliable routines, reduced travel burden.
- Pain. E-prescription confusion, travel difficulty, low confidence with complex UI.
- Needs. Step-by-step prescription flow, clear confirmations, routine telemedicine follow-ups.
- Usage. 1-2x/week, older Android, mornings.

## Elena. Young Wellness and Beauty Enthusiast (23)

- Motivations. Affordable access, convenience, social-proof expectations.
- Pain. Expensive dermatology services, uncertainty, need discretion for sensitive meds.
- Needs. Modern UI, discreet ordering, quick consults for dermatology and skincare.
- Usage. 4-5x/week, iPhone, evenings and weekends.

## Thomas. Value-Driven Shopper (51)

- Motivations. Save money and time. Consolidate trips.
- Pain. Missed discounts, inefficiency, complexity across programs.
- Needs. Transparent pricing, simple confirmations, functional flows. Telemedicine secondary.
- Usage. 2-3x/week, Android, evenings and weekends.
