# Research Recommendations (Condensed)

## Product Emphasis

- Telemedicine is a core differentiator. Treat as flagship.
- Prescription ordering must be seamless and step-by-step.
- Integrate with existing dm surfaces. Loyalty, click and collect, in-store services, online pharmacy.

## Accessibility

- Large readable fonts.
- High contrast.
- Simple navigation.
- Consider guidance for complex tasks.
