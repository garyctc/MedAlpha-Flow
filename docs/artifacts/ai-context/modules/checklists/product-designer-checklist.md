# Product Designer Checklist (Condensed)

## UX and Accessibility

- Meet BITV 2.0 and WCAG 2.1 AA. Contrast, scaling, touch targets.
- Validate layouts across 14+ languages. No overflow.
- Prefer plain language. Explain unavoidable medical terms inline.
- Enforce design system consistency across partner co-branding.
- Keep flows linear. Reduce cognitive load for seniors.

## Workflow and Safety

- Add validation and confirmations for critical actions.
- Ensure error prevention and recovery in high-stakes flows.

## Compliance Touchpoints

- Transparent consent UX aligned with GDPR.
- Legal labels and policy access are visible and discoverable.
